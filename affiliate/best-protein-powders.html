Protein Powder Buying Guide

How to Choose the Right Protein

1. Determine Your Goals

Muscle building, weight loss, or general health? Different goals require different protein types.

2. Consider Dietary Restrictions

Whey for dairy-tolerant, plant-based for vegans, isolate for lactose intolerance.

3. Check Protein Content

Look for 20-25g of protein per serving with minimal carbs and fats.
