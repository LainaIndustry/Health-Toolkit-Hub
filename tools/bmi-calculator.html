About BMI

Body Mass Index (BMI) is a measure of body fat based on height and weight. It helps categorize individuals into underweight, normal weight, overweight, or obese categories.

BMI Categories:

  • Underweight: BMI less than 18.5
  • Normal weight: BMI 18.5 - 24.9
  • Overweight: BMI 25 - 29.9
  • Obese: BMI 30 or greater
