Understanding Macronutrients

Macronutrients are the nutrients your body needs in large amounts: protein, carbohydrates, and fats. Each plays a unique role in your health and fitness goals.

Protein (4 calories per gram)

Essential for muscle repair, immune function, and satiety. Important for muscle building and weight loss.

Carbohydrates (4 calories per gram)

Primary energy source for your body and brain. Important for athletic performance and recovery.

Fats (9 calories per gram)

Essential for hormone production, brain health, and absorbing fat-soluble vitamins.
