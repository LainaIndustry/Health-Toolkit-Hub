Importance of Proper Hydration

Water is essential for nearly every bodily function. Proper hydration helps with digestion, nutrient absorption, temperature regulation, and cognitive function.

Signs of Dehydration

  • Thirst and dry mouth
  • Fatigue and dizziness
  • Dark yellow urine
  • Headaches
  • Dry skin
